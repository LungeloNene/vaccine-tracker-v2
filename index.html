Africa Data Hub and the Media Hack Collective sourced the data for this dashboard from countries’ press releases, reputable news sources, the World Health Organisation and Gavi. Although we do our best to bring you updated data, sometimes there are reporting delays of several days.

We have noted that Rwanda has administered more vaccines than it has received. We are working on why this may be the case.

In the case of Algeria, where the donation variable is represented as a negative, that is indicative of Algeria’s donation to Tunisia.
